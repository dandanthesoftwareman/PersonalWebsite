Who I Am

My name is Daniel Schuler. I'm a born-and-raised Michigander currently residing in Potterville, MI. I have been a musican since I picked up a bass at 14; because I've stuck to playing it I've been able to travel across the mitten to see the beauty of the state, playing shows all along the way. I love camping, hiking, finding good restaurants, a good craft beer, and working with my hands. When I'm not coding I'm generally doing house projects or out playing music with my friends.

Like I said, I've been a musician since I was 14. I started pursing a career in music after my first paid performance and in 2020 it became my fulltime job. I currently play in my original band Pajamas and with a wedding band organization called the Bluewater Kings Band. I also play various concerts with local Michigan-based artists when they need a bass player. I have also worked in recording studios, engineered for churches, built studio spaces, and given lessons as a part of working in the music industry.

I made a transition to developing after a long winter season without gigs to play. I started to look into lines of work that would still allow me to play shows and tour. After ruling out jobs that required the skills I already had such as construction, electronics repair, painting, and clerical work, I turned to an old journal where I had written out a list of things I wanted to eventually learn in life. Somewhere on that list was "learn to program a computer". Next thing I knew I was asking anyone I knew that worked in the tech field about their job and what it was like to work it. My roomate at the time was a Grand Circus alumnus who helped talk me through my first few basic programs, I applied to the C# .Net bootcamp, and the rest is history.
